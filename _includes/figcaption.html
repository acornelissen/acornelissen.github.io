{%- comment -%} Renders a two-voice figcaption from a caption string of the form "Subject [Camera, Lens - Film]". The subject is set upright; the gear list is set smaller, italic, and grey on its own line. A caption with no bracket is treated as subject only. {%- endcomment -%} {%- assign _cap = include.caption -%} {%- if _cap contains "[" -%} {%- assign _subject = _cap | split: "[" | first | strip -%} {%- assign _gear = _cap | split: "[" | last | replace: "]", "" | strip | replace: ", ", " · " | replace: " - ", " · " -%} {%- else -%} {%- endif -%}
